The App

Why join?

Connecting buyers, sellers, and couriers in New Haven with INSTANT sales and deliveries.

Are you a buyer or seller on Yale Free and For Sale tired of coordinating your item's delivery and payment? Wish you could sit back, relax, and have the item delivered to your door for a small fee? Or are you a busy student or young professional who wouldn't mind a little extra cash? Pick up an item near you and make a delivery on your way across New Haven and get paid for it as a courier for Saleboat.

Subscribe now to try out our beta app and be among the first to join the Saleboat sell, shop & delivery community when we go live.
